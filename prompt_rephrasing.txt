* vague pronouns;
* unnecessary repetition;
* excessive signposting;

* long noun strings;

14. Use first-person plural only when it serves a genuine authorial function. Otherwise, prefer direct analytical statements.

15. Do not make the prose artificially complex. Publication-level writing should be precise, economical, and readable.
